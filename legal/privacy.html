Privacy Policy

At PEPSVAL, your privacy and professional data are extremely important. We are a maritime trust and verification platform designed for seafarers, employers, and maritime professionals worldwide.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, rank, sea service records, certificates, employer history, and any documents you choose to upload.

How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to provide verification, professional profiles, employment confirmation, and trusted networking inside the PEPSVAL platform.

Your Control

Your profile data, documents, and sea service are not shared publicly without your consent. You choose what is visible and what is private.

Verification & Locking

Once a sea service or document is verified by an employer or peer, it becomes locked to prevent tampering and ensure trust.

Data Security

We use secure systems and industry-standard protections to safeguard your data from unauthorized access.

Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. Verification requests may be sent only to employers or peers you approve.

Your Rights

You may request to view, correct, or delete your data at any time.

Contact

If you have any privacy questions, contact us at: support@pepsval.com

1 How it works
  1. Create your Pepsval profile (Seafarer Passport).
  2. Upload documents (CoC, passport, medical, visas, etc.).
  3. Add sea time (vessel, rank, dates, company/agency).
  4. Request confirmations from companies or seniors you sailed with.
  5. Get a clear status: Self-declared → Verified/Confirmed.
2 Why Pepsval exists

Hiring is global, but trust is fragmented. Pepsval aims to make seafarer history portable and verifiable — so honest seafarers win more opportunities and companies reduce risk.

Pepsval is building trust signals, not replacing official flag-state processes.

Document Verified
Admin/partner checks document format, authenticity signals, expiry, and consistency.
Employment Confirmed
Employer confirms dates/vessel/rank via a simple “Yes/No” confirmation flow.
Peer Confirmed
A senior officer confirms you “served together” — optional, but powerful.
